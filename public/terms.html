Booking Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

1. Bookings & Deposits

To secure a date, a 50% deposit is required. The remaining balance is due 48 hours prior to the event setup.

2. Cancellations

  • Cancellations made more than 7 days in advance: Full refund of deposit.
  • Cancellations made less than 7 days in advance: Deposit is non-refundable.

3. Site Requirements

The client is responsible for ensuring the venue has:

  • A flat surface of at least 4.6m (width) x 5.5m (depth).
  • Access to a standard power outlet within 30m.
  • A ceiling height of at least 3.3m (for indoor events).
If we arrive and the site is unsuitable, the full fee is still applicable.

4. Liability & Damage

Sim2U is not liable for injury sustained by guests while using the equipment. The Client is responsible for any damage caused to the simulator equipment by guests due to negligence or misuse (e.g., hitting the screen with a club).

4. Weather & Safety Policy

Sim2U operates sensitive electronic equipment (launch monitors, projectors, and screens). Due to the nature of this equipment, the following weather policies apply:

  • Wind Conditions: Operations will be suspended if sustained winds exceed 30km/h or if gusts pose a risk to the structural integrity of the simulator setup.
  • Rain & Moisture: As a mobile outdoor/semi-outdoor service, we cannot operate in active rain or high-moisture conditions. In the event of rain, equipment must be powered down and covered immediately.
  • Provider Discretion: Sim2U reserves the sole right to determine if weather conditions are safe for operation. This decision is made with the safety of the participants and the protection of the equipment as the priority.

4.1 Cancellations & Rescheduling

If an event is cancelled due to inclement weather prior to setup, the client will be offered a priority reschedule date or a [Percentage]% refund of the booking fee. If weather forces a shutdown after setup has commenced, the full booking fee remains payable.
