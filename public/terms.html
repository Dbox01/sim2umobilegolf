Last Updated: January 27, 2026

1. Bookings & Deposits

To secure a date, a 50% deposit is required. The remaining balance is due 48 hours prior to the event setup.

2. Cancellations

  • Cancellations made more than 7 days in advance: Full refund of deposit.
  • Cancellations made less than 7 days in advance: Deposit is non-refundable.

3. Site Requirements

The client is responsible for ensuring the venue has:

  • A flat surface of at least 4.5m (width) x 5.5m (depth).
  • Access to a standard power outlet within 30m.
  • A ceiling height of at least 3.2m (for indoor events).
If we arrive and the site is unsuitable, the full fee is still applicable.
